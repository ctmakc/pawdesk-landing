What you're getting

Everything PawDesk includes

Every plan — including the free beta — gives you a complete grooming management system. No crippled free tier designed to frustrate you into upgrading. During beta, you get the entire Pro feature set at no cost, and your feedback shapes what the product becomes.

When paid plans launch, the Free tier will have a 20-client limit so solo groomers who are just starting out can still use PawDesk without paying. If you grow beyond 20 active clients, you'll want the Pro plan — and because you joined during beta, you'll have a locked-in rate that's lower than what new users pay.

The Business plan is for salons and grooming shops where multiple groomers share a client list. It adds team calendars, staff-level invoicing, and revenue reporting so you can see which services and which team members are driving the most income. See the full feature list.

FAQ

Billing questions, answered

Is PawDesk really free right now?
Yes. PawDesk is completely free during our beta period. You get full access to every feature — scheduling, invoicing, client profiles, voice notes, and reminders — with no credit card required. Beta users will receive a locked-in discounted rate when paid plans launch.
Can I cancel at any time?
Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your Pro or Business plan at any time from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
Is there a free trial for the Pro plan?
Beta users get the full Pro feature set for free during the beta period. When paid plans launch, we'll offer a 14-day free trial on the Pro plan so you can test card payment processing and advanced features before committing.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is yours. If you cancel, you can export all your client records, pet profiles, invoices, and notes as a CSV or PDF before your account closes. We retain your data for 30 days post-cancellation in case you change your mind.
What is the Business plan for?
The Business plan is designed for grooming salons with more than one groomer on staff. It adds team scheduling (so each groomer has their own calendar), a shared client database, multi-staff invoice attribution, and priority support.
Do you charge per appointment or per client?
No. PawDesk charges a flat monthly fee. You can book as many appointments as you want and invoice as many times as needed. The only limit on the Free plan is the number of active client accounts (20). Pro and Business have no appointment or invoice limits.
