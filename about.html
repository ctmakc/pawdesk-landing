About

Software for the groomer, not the chain

Most pet-business software is built for multi-location salons with an office manager. PawDesk is built for the person holding the clippers.

PawDesk is mobile grooming software for independent pet groomers. It combines appointment scheduling, digital invoicing, client and pet profiles, automated appointment reminders, and hands-free voice notes in a single phone-first app — replacing the spreadsheets, text threads, and paper diaries most solo groomers use today. It is in private beta and free to use during the beta period.

Why we exist

The gap we're filling

There are roughly a hundred thousand pet groomers across North America, and the large majority are solo operators or two-person shops. They are skilled tradespeople running real businesses — booking, invoicing, remembering that a specific schnauzer needs hypoallergenic shampoo and bites when you touch his hindquarters.

The software market mostly ignores them. The serious tools are built for multi-chair salons and veterinary clinics with front-desk staff: powerful, expensive, and overkill for one person with clippers and a phone. The cheap tools are generic appointment bookers that have no concept of a pet, a coat type, or a groom history. So most solo groomers end up running a real business out of a spreadsheet and their own memory.

PawDesk is the missing middle: a focused, phone-first app that does the handful of things a solo groomer actually needs, does them well, and gets out of the way.

How we build

The principles we hold to

PawDesk is small and early. We would rather say that plainly than dress it up, because the groomers we want as customers can smell marketing fluff from across the room.

  • Honest about stage. PawDesk is in beta. We don't publish review counts or star ratings we haven't earned, and we mark roadmap features as roadmap features — not as things you can use today.
  • Phone-first, offline-first. Groomers work with their hands, often without good signal. Every core action works on a phone, and the schedule, profiles, and voice recorder work with no connection.
  • Your data is yours. Export everything as CSV or PDF at any time. We never hold client records hostage to keep you subscribed.
  • Built with groomers. Every feature traces back to a real frustration a working groomer described to us, not a feature-comparison checkbox.
  • Fair pricing. Free during beta, with an early-adopter rate locked in for the people who help us build it. No per-appointment nickel-and-diming.
Who we are

A small team, in Canada

PawDesk is built by a small independent team in Canada, for the North American pet-care market. We are intentionally not a venture-funded company chasing the enterprise salon-chain market — that market is well served and not who we are building for.

We're at the stage where talking to us directly actually changes the product. If you groom dogs or cats for a living and something here resonates — or if you think we've got something wrong — tell us. The roadmap is genuinely shaped by beta feedback.
