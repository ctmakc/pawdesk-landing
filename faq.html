Frequently asked questions

12 questions groomers ask us most. Straight answers, no fluff.

Everything you want to know about PawDesk

Can't find your answer? Join the waitlist and ask us directly.

1 What is PawDesk?
PawDesk is a mobile CRM built specifically for independent pet groomers. It combines appointment scheduling, digital invoicing, client and pet profiles, voice notes, and automated appointment reminders in a single phone-first app. It replaces the combination of spreadsheets, text messages, and paper diaries that most solo groomers use today. See all features.
2 Is PawDesk free?
Yes. PawDesk is completely free during our beta period. You get access to every feature — scheduling, invoicing, pet profiles, voice notes, and reminders — with no credit card required. When paid plans launch, beta users receive a locked-in discounted rate lower than the standard price. See pricing plans.
3 Do I need to download an app from the App Store?
No. PawDesk works directly in your phone's browser — Safari on iPhone, Chrome on Android. There is nothing to download or install during beta. You can add PawDesk to your phone's home screen for a native app experience (it appears as an icon like any other app), but the App Store and Google Play are not required.
4 Does PawDesk work for mobile groomers who work from a van?
Yes. PawDesk is designed with mobile groomers in mind. The app caches your data locally so it loads fast even with poor signal out in a suburban driveway or rural area. You can view your schedule, access pet profiles, and dictate voice notes offline. Invoices and appointment data sync when you reconnect.
5 Can I accept card payments through PawDesk?
Card payment processing is included in the Pro plan ($19/month), which launches after beta. During beta you can generate professional PDF invoices, track payment status (paid, pending, overdue), and send payment reminders to clients. Stripe-powered card acceptance is coming in the Pro plan.
6 How does PawDesk store pet information?
Each pet gets a dedicated profile containing breed, weight, coat type, known allergies, preferred shampoo and products, temperament notes, vet contact details, and a full chronological history of every appointment, service performed, and groomer note. Photos can be attached to each visit. Everything is searchable — find any pet by name, breed, or owner in seconds.
7 How do the appointment reminders work?
When you book an appointment in PawDesk, the system automatically schedules an email reminder to go to your client 24 hours before. The email includes the date, time, pet's name, service booked, and your business contact info. You customize the message once. It goes out every time without any manual action from you. Beta testers report no-shows dropping by around 80% after enabling reminders.
8 What is the voice notes feature?
After each groom, tap the microphone icon in the pet's profile and speak your post-appointment observations hands-free. PawDesk auto-transcribes the audio and saves the note to the pet's record. Useful for noting mat locations, product reactions, temperament observations, or anything you want to remember for next time — without having to type anything with wet hands.
9 Can I export my data if I leave PawDesk?
Yes. You can export all your client records, pet profiles, appointment history, and invoices as CSV or PDF at any time from your account settings. Your data is yours and we never hold it hostage. If you cancel a paid plan, your export is available for 30 days after your account closes.
10 Is PawDesk good for cat groomers?
Yes. PawDesk works for any independent pet groomer — cat groomers, dog groomers, and eventually other specialties. The pet profile system handles any species, including specific fields for coat length, texture, and grooming frequency. The scheduling, invoicing, and reminder features work identically regardless of what animals you work with.
11 What if I have a grooming salon with multiple staff?
The Business plan ($49/month) supports up to 5 staff members with individual calendars, a shared client database, staff-level invoice attribution, and revenue reports. It is designed for salons where multiple groomers share clients. See the full Business plan. The Free and Pro plans are optimized for solo operators.

Join the waitlist at pawdesk-landing.pages.dev — just your email and your business type. We'll send you access as soon as your spot is ready. You can also try the live demo at demo.pawdesk.app right now — no sign-up required.
