Check the pet profile before the appointment

Tap on any appointment to see the pet's full profile: breed, coat type, preferred products, allergy notes, and the history of every previous visit. You arrive prepared.

3

Leave a voice note after the groom

Hold the mic button, say what you noticed during the groom. PawDesk transcribes it and saves it to Buddy's record automatically. Takes 15 seconds, worth it forever.

Voice Note — Transcribed
New note — Buddy
"Buddy was great today, used the lavender shampoo, his coat is improving but he still has a small mat behind the left ear. Recommend full groom in three weeks."

4

Mark complete and generate the invoice

Tap "Mark complete" on the appointment, then "Generate invoice." A professional PDF is created instantly. Send it by email or share the link — the client pays online.

5

Book the next appointment on the spot

Before Emma leaves, tap "Book next" to schedule Buddy's next groom in 3 weeks. She gets a confirmation email. The appointment drops into your calendar. Done.
