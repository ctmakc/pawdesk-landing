Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

PawDesk ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit pawdesk-landing.pages.dev or submit your email to our waitlist, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect:

  • Your email address — so we can contact you about early access.
  • Your business type (e.g., groomer, dog walker) — so we can tailor communications.
  • Your current tool (optional, free text) — to understand what you're replacing.
  • The date and time of your submission.

We do not collect your name, phone number, address, payment information, or any other personal data through this landing page.

We do not use cookies or analytics trackers on this site. This privacy policy page loads no third-party scripts or fonts. The main landing page uses Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) to load the Inter typeface — this causes your browser to make a request to Google's servers (see Google's privacy policy). No cookies are set by this request, but Google may log basic request metadata.

2. How we use your information

We use your email to:

  • Notify you when PawDesk early access is ready.
  • Send occasional product updates (infrequent — only when there is something meaningful to share).

We will never sell, rent, or share your email with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How we store your information

Waitlist submissions are stored securely using Resend (our email delivery provider, resend.com) or Cloudflare's KV storage. Both are GDPR-compliant infrastructure providers operating under standard contractual clauses where applicable.

We retain waitlist data for a maximum of 24 months or until you unsubscribe, whichever comes first.

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, our legal basis for collecting and using your email is your consent — specifically, your voluntary submission of the waitlist form. You may withdraw consent at any time by emailing us or clicking "unsubscribe" in any email we send you.

5. Canada Anti-Spam Law (CASL)

By submitting the waitlist form, you provide express consent to receive commercial electronic messages (CEMs) from PawDesk. Each email we send includes an unsubscribe mechanism. We will honor unsubscribe requests within 10 business days, as required by CASL.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your data (the "right to be forgotten").
  • Withdraw consent and stop receiving emails at any time.
  • Data portability — receive a copy of your data in a common format.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@pawdesk.app. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Third-party services

This landing page is hosted on Cloudflare Pages (Cloudflare, Inc.). Cloudflare may log basic request metadata (IP address, timestamp, user agent) as part of their infrastructure. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Email delivery and audience management is handled by Resend (Resend, Inc.). See Resend's privacy policy.

8. Children's privacy

This service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted their information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. If we make material changes, we will notify waitlist subscribers by email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

Questions or concerns? Email us at privacy@pawdesk.app.
