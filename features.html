03 — Client & Pet Profiles

Every pet's story, always at hand

Each profile holds the pet's breed, weight, coat type, known allergies, preferred shampoo, temperament notes, vet contact, and a full history of every visit you've done. You can attach before-and-after photos to each groom so there's never a dispute about what was or wasn't done. Client profiles link to all their pets, so if a family brings in two dogs, you have both records open in seconds.

05 — Voice Notes

Dictate post-groom notes hands-free

After a groom your hands are wet, your phone is across the room, and you have about three minutes before the next dog arrives. Typing a detailed note is the last thing you want to do. So don't type. Talk.

Tap the microphone in the pet's profile and speak naturally: "Buddy had a small mat behind his left ear — took a few minutes to work through. Owner mentioned he's been scratching a lot, might want to switch to hypoallergenic shampoo next time. Nails were very long, trimmed all four paws." PawDesk transcribes the audio instantly and saves the note to Buddy's record. By the time you're finished cleaning up, the record is complete.

FAQ

Feature questions, answered

Can I see my full week of appointments at once?

Yes. PawDesk has a week-at-a-glance calendar view that shows all your booked appointments with client name, pet, service type, and time. You can switch between day, week, and month views.

Do appointment reminders go out automatically?

Yes. PawDesk automatically sends email reminders to clients 24 hours before their appointment. You can customize the message. No manual follow-up needed.

Can I store photos of the pets I groom?

Absolutely. Each pet profile supports multiple photos so you can document coat condition before and after each groom. Photos are attached to visit notes for easy reference.

How do voice notes work?

After a groom, tap the microphone in the pet's profile and dictate your observations hands-free. PawDesk auto-transcribes the audio and saves the note to the pet's record immediately.
